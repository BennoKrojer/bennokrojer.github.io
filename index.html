I'm a 3rd-year PhD student in AI/NLP at MILA & McGill in Montreal with Siva Reddy, supported by the Vanier Scholarship. While I am curious about all forms of language grounding (for examples, see Experience Grounds Language paper), I have worked on vision-and-language reasoning in the past two years: How can models reason with pragmatic language and visual nuances? Can we repurpose Stable Diffusion for challenging discriminative tasks? I also want to incorporate questions around ethics and society into my research.
If I had to summarize my research interests (Dec 2023):
  • Vision-and-Language (VL) reasoning and compositionality
  • Text-to-image models (Stable Diffusion): leveraging and evaluating their representations
  • Creatively stress-testing models
  • Video + Language
  • VL + ethics/society
  • VL creativity, humor, storytelling


Aside from research, I have organized the NewInML workshop at NeurIPS 2023 and founded a reading group on language grounding at Mila. In my undergrad, I also founded a philosophy society which is still thriving to this day! I strongly believe these kinds of activities next to publishing papers are crucial to a healthy and vibrant research community.

Before coming to Mila, I graduated from LMU Munich in computational linguistics where I worked on relational reasoning and machine translation. I also had great experiences at early-stage startups during and after my undergrad!

In my free time, I play Ultimate Frisbee competetively and have coached juniors and a mixed-gender team. With the PhD, I don't play on the highest level anymore but look back on great memories from U24 Worlds with Team Germany and several European Club Championships.
